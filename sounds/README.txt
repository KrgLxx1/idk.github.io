PLACEHOLDER FOR SOUND FILES

To complete this website, you'll need to add the following sound files to this directory:

1. blue-tractor-song.mp3 - A song about a blue tractor (синий трактор едет к нам)
2. jumpscare-sound.mp3 - A scary sound for the jumpscare effect

You can find a suitable blue tractor song by searching for "синий трактор едет к нам" online.
For the jumpscare sound, look for a sudden loud sound effect.
